Content Strategy

My site will be one page, and will consist of the following.

Sidebar: A sidebar with an image of our horse. It will have his name on the upper center and a few brief lines detailing his age and winnings.

About Our Stud: There will be a right-aligned image of our horse with a centered title and left-aligned paragraphs going into detail about him and why he's a good choice for breeding.

Contact Form: On a different background color to make it stand out will be a contact form that asks for a name, an email address, and a message that will route to our email when submitted.
